In support of the Vision, our mission is to:

  • Create, apply and transfer knowledge and technology of international standard through Professional Career Education Programs.
  • Serve and empower the community by meeting their educational and socio-economic development desires and support the national vision of an educated and informed nation by 2016 and beyond.
  • Establish and maintain a strategic partnership network locally and internationally for the mutual benefit of the Institution and all stakeholders including and more especially our learners
